Aw, shoot

Dog Park,Photos,Puppy Class 17 February 2011 | 1 Comment

Roxy is still awesome!
In case there was any doubt.
She’s a bit older, a bit calmer, better at being taken to people’s houses.

The relationship between here and the cats continues to improve, especially with Meep. They often up end sleeping in the same bit of the house and don’t bother each other. Sproing is more… complicated.

We wanted to go back and go the next level of puppy class, but the class was cancelled because not enough people were interested :(.
The location and time and all that were perfect, so hopefully we’ll be able to catch the next wave.

Morning dog park jaunts are as fun as ever. Cool people, cool dogs, lovely way to start the day.

Have some pictures of Roxy from last weekend, on Kommetjie beach.
